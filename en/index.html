The World of KUKU

The above-ground "Tree Village" and the underground "Suikon District".
A mystical story woven by two worlds.

🌳 The Tree Village

The above-ground settlement where the KUKUs live. Giant ancient trees stand tall in a beautiful landscape in harmony with nature.

🌌 Suikon District

A mystical world of roots spreading far beneath the Tree Village. A sanctuary where forest memories accumulate and ancient wisdom and secrets sleep.

♾️

Cycle and Harmony

The above-ground and underground are connected by invisible threads of life, supporting each other to prosper for thousands of years.

📋 Application Requirements

Theme

"Connection"
We are looking for works based on the core theme of the KUKU worldview: "Creators from around the world connect and create together." Interpretation is free.

Submission Requirements

  • Video length must be 60 seconds or less (including titles and credits)
  • Must use KUKU characters or worldview
  • Must be an original work (fan works OK, plagiarism from other works not allowed)
  • Any language is acceptable

Production Methods

All methods are welcome!

  • Hand-drawn animation
  • AI utilization
  • 3DCG (Blender, Unreal Engine, etc.)
  • Live2D / Stop motion / Live action (cosplay, etc.)
  • Combinations of the above

Eligibility

  • No restrictions on age, nationality, professional or amateur status
  • Individual or team submissions accepted
  • Multiple submissions per person (or team) allowed
